Press Bio (100 words)

Christopher D. Leonard is a speculative fiction author. Some of his works are available at Daily Science Fiction and Abyss and Apex; and a short story collection, Strange Fiction, arrives later this year. He holds a B. A. in English Literature from the University of Washington, Seattle. Gaming credits include the Dionysian for the Call of Cthulhu RPG. For that same game, a scenario collection, Welcome to Washington, is in the works. He is the author of the WGA-registered screenplay The Worst She Could've Said.
